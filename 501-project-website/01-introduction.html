1. About this project

With rise of social media coupled with the Covid-19 pandemic, cyberbullying has reached all time highs. We can combat this by creating models to automatically flag potentially harmful tweets as well as break down the patterns of hatred. As social media usage becomes increasingly prevalent in every age group, a vast majority of citizens rely on this essential medium for day-to-day communication. Social media’s ubiquity means that cyberbullying can effectively impact anyone at any time or anywhere, and the relative anonymity.

My topic revolves around automating cyberbullying detection by leveraging machine learning methods. Cyberbulleying is a long-exsiting issue that concerns us. During to the Covid-19 pandemic, we rely more often on social media , which might expose us with cyberbullying. My question divides into 10 part, and I would conduct the research experienment to elaborate them.

  • 1. How to define cyberbullying?
  • 2. What kind of aftermath that cyberbullying may lead to us?
  • 3. Does covid-19 pandemic abet cyberviolence or have an impact on it?
  • 4. How many cyberbullying categories are there by roughly calculating?
  • 5. Can we categorize the personalities of the abusers under the text? And people of what kind of personalities are more likely to exert oral violence
  • 6. What kind of Characteristics words or emojis look like in cyberbullying context?(We can recognize them the last time we are confronted with cyberbullying)
  • 7. How to detect the cyberbullying through machine learning methods
  • 8. Which machine learning method outstands?
  • 9. How to interpret the evaluation independent variables that impact cyberbullying level
  • 10. What can we possibly enhance our experienments accuracy, in other words, how can we further employ natural language processing to detect cyberbullying
